ABOUT US

Baila Amor is the Dance Club of School Of Engineering, JNU. We encourage you to take Dance to the next level. We work hard to Enhance your Dance Skills that too with the grace and spark. We conduct/organise/perform in events like "Dance Battle", "Flashmobs", "Freestyle", "Duo Dance", "Fresher's Dances" and many more. So if you want to stand out of the crowd then give our Dancing Community a chance to make you Dance.
