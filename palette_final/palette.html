Palette: We Draw, We Derive          

ABOUT US

Palette - A rigid, flat surface on which a painter arranges and mixes colors and paints the world with imagination and creativity. We are dedicated to promote all aspects of fine arts, including graphic design and visual art. Palette encourages artistic endeavours and brings out the artist in each of us. We believe that art is not limited to just paints and brushes but can be produced out of anything. It is a perfect platform for you to showcase your artistic capabilities and also learn various new art-forms.
