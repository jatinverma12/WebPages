PERSPECTIVES:THE DEBATE CLUB

ABOUT US

Debating club of JNU, SE (School of engineering) empowers students to express themselves clearly and concisely. It also encourages them to explore the power of effective communication while helping them fight Glossophobia. We conduct events like “Extempore debates”, “Poetry recitations”, “Informative discussions”, and many more with an intention to invoke constructive sessions which proves beneficial to all its members. Every session comes loaded with opportunities to work on self. Members of PERSPECTIVES have participated in national events like “National Youth Festival (NYF)” and “National Youth Parliament Festival (NYPF)”. If you are ready to unleash your public persona, this is the right place providing abundant opportunities to students with literary bent.
