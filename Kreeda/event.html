KREEDA : THE SPORTS CLUB

ABOUT US

Welcome to sports club of SE family . Sports must be an essential part of our lifestyle .We here at SE work to keep the students fit and health by organising regular events of football , cricket ,badminton etc. We also conduct 'YUDHH' ,the sports fest ,annually to motivate students towards sports. The club has all the facilities and equipments needed for almost all the popular outdoor and indoor games ranging from cricket,football,basketball etc. to chess,carrom,table-tennis . The club is one of the most active clubs . Join us to stay fit and strong.

EVENTS


YUDHHA

We organise an annual sports fest in the month of November. The event is based on interbranch theme .Teams from every branch come to participate in this fierce battle for pride .We organise a combat in many sports ranging from cricket,football volleyball to badminton ,chess,carrom . We also have competitions like long-jump , sprint ,Tug-of-war, Kho-Kho etc. The winner of every sport gets the shinning trophies and medals.

INTERHOSTEL CRICKET TOURNAMENT

In JNU , campus spread across 1000 acres of land ,hosting youth from different parts of the country and abroad in 18 different hostels , every hostel prepares its cricket team to participate in the INTERHOSTEL CRICKET TOURNAMENT held annually. The winner of the tournaament and the serieseven gets the chance to participate at the higher levels and represent JNU at INTERUNIVERSITY Cricket Tournaments.
